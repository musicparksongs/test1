My Love, My Fairest
I Am Yours, Ever True.


I am a singing poet and philosopher, principal baritone
who has performed leading roles in opera in Italy and Korea.

I have established my musical foundation over years of recording and mastering over 1,000 works—spanning Opera, global Art Songs, and K-Classic repertoire. Now, I silence all external demands to embark on a new phase of creation: composing the deepest dialogue with my own soul.

My compositions oscillate between lyrical intimacy and dramatic intensity. Rooted in the power of an Opera Principal's voice, this music functions as a conversation with Love and Soul, explored through two distinct directions:

Intimate Confessions: A private dedication and dialogue with the person I loved most, structured within the emotional framework of K-Classic.

Vast Explorations: Philosophical and thematic explorations, including admiration for the cosmos and nature and humanity's destiny (often in Latin, Italian, and English).

I am committed to transcending all conventions. Leveraging the expertise and power of an Opera Principal's voice, this Bandcamp channel will exclusively document the journey to establish my own 'New Tonality'—deconstructing and recreating all genres purely through my original work.
